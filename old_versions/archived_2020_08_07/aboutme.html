About Me

I am an undergrad at Carnegie Mellon University, majoring in Statistics with a minor in Computer Science. My passion lays in web development and data visualization, and I really enjoy (and good at) hacking. I love the process of ideating, designing, prototyping, and engineering in such a short span of time with my teammates. In the coming fall, I am looking to begin my senior thesis in data cleaning and visualization (proposal here), as well as research with Professor Niki Kittur in CMU HCI Institute.

There is something beautiful in the notion of giving life to ideas, and being an engineer gives me the power to do so — and I love it.
